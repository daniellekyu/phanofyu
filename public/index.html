our story

Our story began in the most romantic place. The parking lot at the University of California, Irvine. The part of the story that Danielle likes to mention is that Minh popped out of a bush with his pick-up line "Hey, do you know where I can find parking?" It turns out that both of our friends decided to have a joint birthday party together and each friend invited us. We struck up a conversation at the party and kept in touch. A few months later Minh asked Danielle out on their first date and the rest, as they say, is history.

travel

by air

There are three airports in the Bay Area. The closest airport to our venue is San Jose Airport (SJC). San Jose Airport also allows Uber and Lyft ridesharing apps in addition to taxis to get you where you want to be.

staying here

There are several options for your stay in the Bay Area. We've reserved a small block of hotel rooms at the Juniper Hotel in Cupertino. The hotel can be located at 10050 S De Anza Blvd, Cupertino, CA 95014 which is 15 minutes away from the venue. Please mention then Yu-Phan Wedding when booking. Also you'd like to take advantage of the tech capital you can also book an entire home using airbnb or vrbo.

getting around

The Bay Area is a beautiful place during the fall. We've put together a guide of a few of our favorite places for you to enjoy. There are multiple ways to get around if you do not have a car. CalTrain, BART and MUNI connect much of Bay Area.
